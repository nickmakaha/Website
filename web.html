Work & Experience
PolyArch

PolyArch is a computer architecture lab at UCLA focusing on developing novel ISAs, accelerator architectures and their compilers, accelerator design-automation techniques, and developing open-source architecture design frameworks.

My main project as part of PolyArch is to implement a conditional step instruction in a simulated Gem5 MicroSuite. In my preliminary work, I wrote Python scripts to generate matrices of varying sparsities. I then utilized a modified version of GAP to benchmark a ternary and a branching implementation of a dot product on said sparse matrices. This showed feasibiility of a conditional step showing significant performance increases when compared to a traditional step instruction.
